Security and Compliance Information

SOC 2 Type II Compliance

Our organization adheres to SOC 2 Type II standards, ensuring that we manage your data with the highest security and confidentiality levels. This involves regular audits and assessments by third-party assessors to validate our processes and controls.

Encryption Standards

We employ robust encryption methods to protect data both in transit and at rest. This includes:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest.
  • TLS 1.2 or higher for data in transit.

Zero-Trust Networking

Our infrastructure operates on a zero-trust philosophy, requiring verification for each request regardless of the source. This minimizes the risk of threats and ensures only authenticated users gain access to sensitive data.

Hardware Attestation

We implement hardware attestation to verify the integrity and origin of hardware devices accessing our services. This helps ensure that only trusted devices can interact with our systems.

Automated Key Rotation

Keys used for encryption and access are rotated automatically on a regular basis to mitigate the risk of unauthorized access and increase overall system security.

DDoS Mitigation

We have strategies in place to mitigate DDoS attacks, including traffic analysis, anomaly detection, and the use of cloud-based DDoS protection services.
